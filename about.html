About Rikki

Compassionate, science-based coaching that meets you where you are.

Your Coach

Hi, I'm Rikki White

I started Best New Life Nutrition because I believe that real, lasting change happens when coaching goes beyond the food on your plate. Too often, nutrition advice ignores the full context of a person's life — the stress, the sleep deprivation, the inconsistent movement patterns. I wanted to do something different.

My approach is science-based and data-driven, but it's also deeply personal. I take the time to understand where you are, what you're struggling with, and what success looks like for you — then we build a plan that's actually livable.

The goal isn't a perfect diet for six weeks. The goal is for you to understand your own nutrition well enough to make confident choices for the rest of your life.

  • Certified Nutrition Coach
  • Specialization in body composition and metabolic health
  • Science-based, data-driven methodology
  • Personalized programs tailored to your lifestyle
  • Ongoing check-ins and accountability
