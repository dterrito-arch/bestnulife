Common Questions

FAQs

What happens on a discovery call?

We spend about 20–30 minutes talking about your goals, current habits, and what you're looking for in a coach. There's no pitch and no obligation — it's just a conversation.

Is coaching done in person or online?

All coaching is conducted remotely, so you can work with me no matter where you're located. Sessions happen via video call, with ongoing support through messaging.

How long until I see results?

Most clients notice meaningful changes within the first 4–8 weeks, though it depends on your starting point and goals. We set realistic expectations from day one.

Do I need to track everything I eat?

Not necessarily. We'll figure out the right level of tracking for you — some clients prefer detailed logging, others do better with a more flexible framework.
